Who we are

  • Now is your chance to come in and enjoy a traditional Korean meal cooked by you or have our seasoned cooking staff prepare your meal just the way you want it. When you arrive at New Seoul Korean Barbeque Restaurant you will be greeted by a warm welcoming staff and environment.
  • You grill your meal right in the middle of your own marble table, as well as, served fresh Korean side dishes with every meal. The experience is one of a kind and hard to beat!
  • The owners, Tom Kim & Un Sook Shin, as well as, the seasoned staff have been providing excellent service for over 20years

About Us

  • New Seoul is regarded as one of the best Korean Barbeque Restaurants in the Des Plaines-Chicago area. The smell, flavor, freshness, and traditional Korean Barbeque experience is just the beginning.
  • Since 1991 Tom Kim & Un Sook Shin has offered mouthwatering, authentic and exceptionally presentable Korean dishes to the Des Plaines-Chicago area. We only use daily prepared highest quality fresh meats and vegetables.
  • Un Sook Shin started working in New Seoul restaurant over 30 years ago, and in 1991 had become the owner. Since 1991 New Seoul has been in the Daily Harold as well as other leading newspapers for providing excellent service.
